CONGRATULATIONS!

closing remarks from the author

congratulations. you made it through the puzzles (if you can even call them that). in doing so, you have shown your creativity and ability to, as my seventh-grade math teacher always said, "think outside of the triangle".

some might say that mezclo ramo is "weird", "stupid", or "nonsensical". i very much disagree. mezclo ramo is simply a playground in every sense of the word.

as young children, we often took for granted how incredibly vast our imaginations were. imaginary friends waltzed around the hallways, a dinosaur ate a spiderman action figure, robots could shoot lasers and take over the world.

all of this imagination culminated in that most lively gathering spot, the playground.

the purpose of mezclo ramo is to neither make sense nor refuse to make sense; rather, it is intended to bring the player back to the playground of old, where the rules were yours to make.

as it is, i hope you find your playground. i hope you are able to recreate the environment of your childhood.

take care!
